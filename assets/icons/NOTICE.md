Language selector icon assets:

- `globe.png` is generated from Font Awesome Free's globe icon. Font Awesome Free icons are licensed under CC BY 4.0, with code under MIT.
- `flag-gb.png`, `flag-ae.png`, `flag-ru.png`, and `flag-in.png` are generated from the `flag-icons` project, licensed under MIT.

The PNG files are vendored locally so the language menu works without third-party icon requests.
